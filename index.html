Candle Making Guide

Materials

  • Wax (soy, beeswax, or paraffin)
  • Wicks
  • Jars or molds
  • Fragrance oils
  • Dye (optional)

Steps

  1. Melt your wax safely using a double boiler.
  2. Attach the wick to the bottom of your jar.
  3. Add fragrance and color to the melted wax.
  4. Pour the wax into your container and center the wick.
  5. Let it cool and trim the wick to Â¼ inch.

Scents

Popular scents include lavender, vanilla, cinnamon, eucalyptus, and sandalwood. Try blending your own combinations!
